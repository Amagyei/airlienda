About Us

Welcome to Liendaville, the premier student hostel in Ayeduase, Kumasi, Ghana, catering specifically to the vibrant community of KNUST students. At Liendaville, we strive to create a comfortable and conducive living environment that supports both academic and personal growth. Our hostel is equipped with modern facilities including a standby generator to ensure uninterrupted power supply, well-equipped gyms to promote a healthy lifestyle, dedicated study rooms for focused learning, and inviting common rooms for social interactions. Our spacious rooms are designed to provide a homely feel, complete with semi-furnished kitchens that offer the convenience of preparing your own meals. Whether you're looking for a quiet place to study, a space to work out, or a community to connect with, Liendaville offers it all. Join us at Liendaville and experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and community.
